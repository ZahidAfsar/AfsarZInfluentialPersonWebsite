Facts About Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru has 5 main abilites which are Six Eyes, Limitless, Red, Blue, and Hollow Purple. The Six Eyes is a rare inherited gift unique to the Gojo clan, granting the user incredible perception and control over cursed energy. The Limitless is a powerful technique that allows Satoru Gojo to manipulate space, making him virtually invincible against attacks. The technique grants Gojo the ability to stop any attack directed at him and generate strong attractive or repulsive forces to overpower opponents. Red, or Reversed Limitless, reverses Limitless through the concept of divergence, generating the power to repel. This technique can be used to unleash immense explosive force in an instant. Blue is achieved when the Limitless user greatly amplifies the amount of cursed energy poured into the technique. This brings the concepts of "negative numbers" and "negative distance" into reality, forcing real space to compensate and fill in the area by drawing everything toward the impossibility. This generates a strong force of attraction, similar to a powerful magnet. Hollow Purple is the fusion of both Red and Blue. This produces an imaginary mass which erases everything in its path from reality.
